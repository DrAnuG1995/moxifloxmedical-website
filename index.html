Approach

Where clinical judgement meets commercial reality.

Health is the most regulated, highest-stakes industry there is. Good intentions and a clever product aren’t enough. The work has to survive contact with clinicians, patients, payers, and regulators. That’s the brief.

01  Clarify

Before anything is built or pitched, the clinical claim has to hold. We start by stress-testing the assumption: what problem, for whom, and what evidence makes it true.

02  Operate

Workflow design, governance frameworks, clinician adoption, incident response, regulatory posture. The unglamorous work that decides whether a product lives or dies in production.

03  Translate

Clinical language for boards. Commercial language for clinicians. Regulatory language for both. Most failures in healthtech are translation failures. We close that gap.

About

Built by a clinician who has shipped product.

Dr Anurag Ganugapati is a doctor and healthcare operator working across Australian healthcare, digital health, and medical workforce strategy. Moxiflox Medical is his advisory practice.

Years of full-time locum work across metro, rural, and remote hospitals gave him a working view of the system’s operational pressure points: staff shortages, inefficient workforce management, rising locum costs, and the widening gap between clinicians and administration.

As founder of StatDoctor, he built one of Australia’s emerging healthcare workforce platforms, focused on changing how hospitals and doctors connect, reducing reliance on traditional agencies, improving workforce sustainability, and designing better systems for both sides of the roster.

Alongside that, he advises healthcare businesses, startups, and medical service providers on growth strategy, operations, workforce design, and navigating the realities of the Australian healthcare landscape. The throughline: frontline clinical insight, commercial execution, and system-level thinking, in the same room.
