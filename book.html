Before you book

What happens after I book?
You'll receive a Stripe receipt, a calendar invite (with the video link), and a short brief asking what you'd like to cover. The more context you share, the more useful the call.
Can I reschedule or cancel?
The calendar invite includes a reschedule link, with no fee to move once. Cancellations made at least 2 days (48 hours) before the booked time are refunded in full. Cancellations inside that window, and no-shows, are non-refundable.
How do you handle confidentiality?
Every call is treated as confidential by default. Anything you share, whether it's clinical, commercial, product, or personal, is held in confidence and not discussed externally. Conflicts of interest are checked in advance. If you'd like a specific NDA signed before we speak, add a note to your booking and include a link to the document, and it will be reviewed and returned before the call.
Do you offer pro bono or reduced-rate sessions?
A small number of sessions each quarter are reserved for early-stage founders, junior clinicians, and public-sector teams without budget. Reach out via LinkedIn with a sentence on what you're working on.
